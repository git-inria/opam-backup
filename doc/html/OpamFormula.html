type t = (OpamPackage.Name.t * (string * OpamPackage.Version.t) formula)
       formula
An atom is: name * (relop * version) formula. Examples of valid formulaes:
  • "foo" {> "1" & (<"3" | ="5")}
  • "foo" {= "1" | > "4"} | ("bar" "bouh")
